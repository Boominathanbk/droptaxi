DROP TAXI BOOKING – Terms of Use

By booking a ride through our platform, you agree to the following terms and conditions.

1. Booking & Travel Rules

  • Kilometers are calculated from actual pickup location.
  • AC may not function in hill areas or when vehicle is stationary.
  • Bookings operate on calendar day basis (12 AM – 11:59 PM).
  • Outstation rides cannot be converted to local trips after return.
  • Calendar Day Basis: All bookings operate strictly on a calendar-day basis (12:00 AM to 11:59 PM). If a cab is booked at 3:00 PM on the 14th, the booking will expire at 11:59 PM on the 14th, and not 24 hours from the pickup time unless explicitly agreed otherwise.

2. Luggage & Seating Capacity

The platform enforces strict limits based on vehicle type. Exceeding the permitted passenger or luggage capacity may result in additional charges at the driver's discretion or possible ride cancellation.

3. Payments & Cancellation

  • Payment is generally made directly to the driver.
  • ₹300 cancellation charge if vehicle has reached pickup location.
  • Customer must pay tolls, parking, state taxes & night charges.

4. Delays & Modifications

  • Booking changes must be requested at least 10 hours before pickup.
  • Waiting charges apply after 15 minutes delay.

5. Liability Disclaimer
